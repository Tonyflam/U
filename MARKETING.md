### Under 1000 users by Day 20

## Things NOT to do

- Don't post the same thing twice in the same day with different wording. People notice.
- Don't tag accounts >100k followers and beg for RT. They block you and the algo punishes you.
- Don't post in HL community groups without admin permission. Single best way to get banned.
- Don't shitpost competitors. Mention them factually in fee tables, never in tweets.
- Don't ever say "guaranteed," "risk-free," "X% APY," "will moon." HL itself blocks these terms in some channels.
- Don't ship new features mid-week. If something breaks, you've split your focus on the most critical week.
