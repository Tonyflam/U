How it works

Three steps. Two signatures. No deposits.

Connect your wallet

MetaMask, Rabby, Coinbase, Trust, or any of 380+ wallets via WalletConnect. Inside Telegram, on your phone.

Approve agent + fee

Two off-chain EIP-712 signatures register a Hyperliquid agent for trading and authorize a small builder fee. No on-chain transaction, no gas.

Pick your whales

Browse the curated list, set per-whale caps, and you're mirroring. Pause, resume, or kill anytime.

Security

What WhalePod will never do.

Non-custodial means non-custodial. We engineer it so we can't betray you even if we wanted to.

  • Move funds off Hyperliquid. Agent keys are scoped to placing and canceling orders. Withdraw is impossible by protocol design.
  • Charge above the cap. The fee is fixed at 5.0 bps per fill and enforced on-chain — it cannot exceed Hyperliquid's 10.0 bps hard cap, ever.
  • Hold your funds. Your equity stays in your Hyperliquid account. WhalePod never has a balance to drain.
  • Hide the code. Open-source on GitHub. Audit the verifier, the risk caps, the kill path.
